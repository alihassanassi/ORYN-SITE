UNDER THE HOOD

What KAELEN actually does on your network.

This is the production sensor that runs 24/7 inside your perimeter. No marketing fluff — this is the real architecture.

01

Packet-Level Capture

Passive tap between your firewall and managed switch. Full protocol analysis across DNS, HTTP/S, SMB, RDP, and SSH. Circular buffer — no raw payloads stored, only metadata and evidence hashes.

02

8 Detection Engines

Port scanning (T1046), brute force (T1110), DNS tunneling (T1071.004), C2 beacon detection (T1071), lateral movement (T1021), data exfiltration (T1048), ARP spoofing (T1557.002), rogue DHCP (T1557.003). All MITRE ATT&CK mapped.

03

TLS Fingerprinting

JA3/JA3S extraction from every TLS handshake. Checked against 50+ known-malicious fingerprints: Cobalt Strike, Emotet, Trickbot, Metasploit, and more. Custom fingerprints addable per client.

04

Data Loss Prevention

Outbound DLP scanning for Social Security numbers, credit card numbers (with Luhn validation), bulk email addresses, credentials in cleartext, and file signature extraction. No raw data stored — only hashes and match counts.

05

Behavioral Baselines

Every IP gets a rolling 24-hour behavioral profile: byte/packet volumes, destination IPs, protocols, active hours. Z-score anomaly detection at 2.5 sigma catches zero-day behavior that signature-based tools miss entirely.

06

Multi-Tenant MSSP

Client-isolated telemetry. Every event tagged with sensor ID. Cross-client correlation is architecturally impossible — your data is scoped at the database layer. WireGuard tunnels for remote management.
