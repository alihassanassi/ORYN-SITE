TECHNOLOGY

Powered by machine intelligence.

ORYN isn't just another dashboard. It's an AI that thinks, learns, and adapts — running locally on your network with zero cloud dependency.

01

Firewall Behind the Firewall

ORYN sits inside your network perimeter as a passive sensor. Full packet capture, deep packet inspection, anomaly detection — invisible to attackers.

02

AI Threat Correlation

Our AI engine correlates events across your entire infrastructure in real-time. It doesn't just alert — it understands context, reduces false positives, and prioritizes what matters.

03

Zero Cloud Dependency

Your data never leaves your building. ORYN runs entirely on local hardware — no cloud subscriptions, no data exfiltration risk, no third-party access.

04

Continuous Learning

ORYN gets smarter every day. It studies the latest CVEs, exploit techniques, and threat intelligence overnight — so it's always ahead of emerging attacks.

05

7-Gate Safety Chain

Every automated action passes through 7 sequential safety gates: scope, policy, operator approval, rate limit, audit log, kill switch, and sandbox. No exceptions.

06

Human in the Loop

AI augments your security — it doesn't replace human judgment. Every critical decision requires operator approval. Full audit trail. Kill switch always armed.
